• Energy Management System
    • The project has two major modules Remote Energy Monitoring(REM) and Bill Management System(BMS). I work on REM module.
    • REM module is responsible for data capturing and analysis to help the client understand their energy needs and manage them.
    • I work on various dashboards and reports to showcase the energy profile of various parameters e.g. electricity consumption, gas consumption, temperature, etc. to the client.
  • Detection of Multiple Traffic Signals in an Image Using Convolutional Neural Networks on MATLAB
    • In this research project a transfer learning approach is used. At first the network is trained on CIFAR-10 dataset consisting of 50000 training images to classify objects. The network is implemented using various number of hidden layers and accuracy of classification is recorded.
    • After that the network is trained again on a comparatively smaller dataset consisting of 200 images containing five traffic signs to detect these traffic signs in test images.
  • Industrial Automation System
    • This system consists of a Robotic arm which is controlled by AVR (Atmega16) microcontroller. The robotic arm is equipped with a gripper and driller attached to its end-effector.
    • The system consists of a conveyor belt mechanism also. This mechanism is controlled by PLC (Allen-Bradley Micrologix 1000). This system sorts objects on the basis of their size and their metallic character.
